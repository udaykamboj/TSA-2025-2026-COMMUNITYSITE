## 1. Request a certificate {#request}

Request a certified copy of a birth certificate online, by mail, or in person.
Certified copies are required for passports, school, and benefits. 

:::custom-card{title="Fastest Option" buttonText="Request Online Now" buttonLink="/request-birth-certificate"}
Online requests are the fastest way to get your certificate. Upload your ID, pay the fee, and receive it by mail.
:::

## 2. Eligibility {#eligibility}

You can request your own certificate, or your child's.
Legal guardians and authorized representatives may also request copies.

:::custom-alert{type="info" title="Proof Required"}
You must provide valid identification and proof of relationship for restricted records.
:::

## 3. Fees and processing {#fees}

Standard processing takes 2 to 6 weeks.
Expedited service is available for an additional fee.

:::custom-dropdown{title="What if the birth was out of state?"}
You must request the certificate from the state where the birth occurred.
:::

:::custom-dropdown{title="Can I get it in Spanish?"}
Certificates are issued in English. You must hire a certified translator if you need it in another language.
:::
