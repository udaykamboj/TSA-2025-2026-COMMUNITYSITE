The Youth Arts Showcase celebrates young artists with live performances, exhibits, and hands-on activities. Bring the family to see dance, music, visual art, and more—and try your hand at creating something new.

## Overview {#overview}

The **Youth Arts Showcase** is a free, family-friendly event at the City Arts Center. Local youth programs, schools, and individual young artists display and perform their work. The afternoon includes a mix of scheduled performances and drop-in activities so you can stay for an hour or the whole event.

## Schedule {#schedule}

- **2:00 PM** — Doors open; visual art exhibits and hands-on activity stations open.
- **2:30 PM** — Welcome and first performance block (dance and music).
- **3:30 PM** — Second performance block; exhibits and activities continue.
- **4:30 PM** — Final performances and closing.
- **5:00 PM–6:00 PM** — Mingling and last chance to visit exhibits and activities.

A detailed program with performer names and room locations is available at the door and on the Arts Center website the week of the event.

## How to participate {#participate}

**As an attendee** — No ticket or registration needed. Show up anytime between 2:00 PM and 6:00 PM. Seating for performances is first-come, first-served.

**As a youth performer or exhibitor** — Registration is handled through participating schools and youth arts programs. If you are part of a program, ask your instructor about signing up. Individual young artists can contact the Arts Center for information on limited slots for the next showcase.

The City Arts Center is at 789 Arts Way. Parking is free in the lot; the center is also on bus route 8.
