Senior Wellness Day offers health screenings, nutrition tips, and connections to resources for older adults. Come for blood pressure checks, fall-prevention information, and one-on-one help with benefits and programs designed for seniors.

## Overview {#overview}

**Senior Wellness Day** is a free event at the North Community Center for older adults and their families. The focus is on prevention, wellness, and linking participants to ongoing services. You can get quick health checks, learn about nutrition and physical activity, and speak with staff about Medicare, SNAP, housing, and other benefits. No registration required; drop in anytime between 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

## Activities {#activities}

- **Health screenings** — Blood pressure and basic wellness checks. Results are explained on the spot; staff can refer you to a primary care provider if needed.
- **Nutrition and exercise** — Short talks and handouts on eating well and staying active as we age. Information on senior meal programs and fitness classes.
- **Fall prevention** — Tips and resources to make your home safer and reduce fall risk.
- **Benefits and programs** — One-on-one or small-group help with Medicare, SNAP, rental assistance, and other programs. Bring any notices or documents you have questions about.
- **Resource tables** — Local agencies and senior services with information and sign-up options.

Language assistance can be arranged; ask at the welcome desk. The center is wheelchair accessible.

## Location and parking {#location-parking}

The event is at the **North Community Center**, 200 North Avenue. Free parking is available in the lot. The center is on bus routes 5 and 11; some routes offer senior fare discounts. The event runs from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Light refreshments are provided.
