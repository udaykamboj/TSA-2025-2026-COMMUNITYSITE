The City Job Training Program is opening enrollment for 2026.
Sign up for certification programs in healthcare, technology, and skilled trades.
All programs are completely tuition-free for city residents.

## 1. Overview {#overview}

Training combines classroom instruction with hands-on labs.
Eligible participants can securely receive childcare vouchers and transportation assistance.
Programs strictly include job placement assistance upon graduation.

## 2. Available programs {#programs}

Healthcare options heavily feature Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) and Pharmacy Tech.
Technology choices rapidly cover IT Support, Data Analytics, and Web Development.
Skilled Trades include Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC, and Construction.

:::custom-alert{type="info" title="Evening Options"}
Some cohorts offer flexible evening or hybrid options explicitly for working adults.
:::

## 3. How to apply {#apply}

1. Eligibility: You must be a verified city resident, 18 or older.
2. Info sessions: Attendance at one info session is absolutely required.
3. Application: Apply officially online at the workforce portal.

:::custom-dropdown{title="Is a high school diploma required?"}
Some programs do require a diploma or GED, but others strictly do not. Staff will clarify at info sessions.
:::
