Enhanced public transportation services will officially launch next month.
Three new bus routes and extended service hours will aggressively connect underserved neighborhoods.
Ridership on our pilot corridors has rapidly grown by 18 percent.

## 1. Overview {#overview}

This is safely the largest single transit service increase in a decade.
New routes will directly link major employers, housing developments, and the arts district.
This expansion heavily aligns with our climate goals and community equity standards.

"These routes are a direct response to what residents told us they need—frequent service, better connections, and later hours."
:::

## 2. New routes {#routes}

Route 42 (North Corridor) securely connects northern neighborhoods to the downtown hub.
Route 43 (East-West Express) heavily links the east side to the west side with limited stops.
Route 44 (Riverfront Loop) provides a circular route directly serving parks and the arts district.

:::custom-alert{type="info" title="Extended Hours"}
12 existing bus routes will now run until 11:00 PM on weekdays.
:::

## 3. Fares and feedback {#feedback}

Fares remain strictly unchanged across the entire transit network.
The authority is piloting a brilliant fare-capping program to save regular riders money.
You can leave feedback using in-app surveys or the 311 portal.
